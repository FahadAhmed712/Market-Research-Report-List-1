<p><strong>The Ribbon Fiber Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ribbon fiber cables market is categorized mainly into single-mode and multi-mode types. Single-mode cables, with a small core diameter, allow for the transmission of light directly down the fiber, enabling long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. Multi-mode cables, on the other hand, feature a larger core and support multiple light modes, making them suitable for shorter distances and higher bandwidth applications, often used in data centers and local area networks. Both types cater to diverse telecom and networking needs.</p></p>
