<p><p>The cellphone display market features various technologies, including IPS (In-Plane Switching) for better color accuracy and wider viewing angles, ASV (Advanced Super View) that enhances brightness and contrast, and TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) known for fast response times. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) offers vibrant colors and deep blacks, while STN (Super Twisted Nematic) and CSTN (Color Super Twisted Nematic) are older technologies, typically used in budget devices, focusing on lower power consumption but with limited color performance.</p></p>
